About me

Hi! I'm Natalie. I’m a 23 year old who recently graduated with a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Creative Writing and a minor in Marketing from Chapman University! I recently moved back to the East Coast after having spent two years in Southern California and I’m enjoying every minute of it. I have a passion for all things writing. My experience lies in screenwriting, short fiction, non-fiction essays, and public relations. I believe all of these mediums of writing allow you to tell a story, which is my absolute favorite thing to do.

A s far as my work experience goes, I’ve held a few positions which have allowed me to hone these story-telling skills. I would call myself a driven, punctual individual who is always excited to take on challenging tasks and learn new skills. I love to problem solve!
